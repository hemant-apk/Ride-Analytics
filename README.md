# Ride-Analytics

**Your private Uber trip dashboard — analyze your data directly in the browser.**

Ride Analytics is a privacy-friendly, client-side tool that helps you understand your Uber ride history in seconds. All data processing happens locally in your browser — your data never leaves your device.

Charts in this tool are powered by **[Google Charts](https://developers.google.com/chart)** for visualizing monthly spend and other analytics.

## Features

- Upload your Uber trips CSV — no login required
- Analyze total spend, ride count, distance, and ride duration
- View monthly spending trends and vehicle usage
- Filter rides by currency and month
- Simple charts and tables for easy analysis powered by **Google Charts**
- Completely client-side and private

---

## How to Download Your Uber Trip Data

1. Go to [Uber Privacy Portal](https://myprivacy.uber.com/exploreyourdata/trips) and sign in with your Uber account.
2. Request your trip data by clicking the download/request option.
3. Wait for the [email from Uber](https://mail.google.com/mail/u/0/#search/from%3Auber.com+Your+Uber+data+is+ready+for+download). Data preparation usually takes **1–2 days**.
4. Download the ZIP file from the email.
5. Extract the ZIP → open the `Rider` folder → use `trips_data-0.csv` for analysis.

---

## Usage

1. Clone or download this repository.
2. Open `index.html` in your browser.
3. Upload your Uber `trips_data-0.csv`.
4. Click **Analyze** to view your ride analytics.
5. Use the filters to view rides by currency or month.

---



## Privacy

Ride Analytics processes all data locally in your browser. **Your data is never sent to a server.**
